About Pinenas

Our story, our promise, and the people behind the pineapple

Our Story

From a small family orchard to your doorstep

Pinenas began in 2018 with a single idea: Malaysia grows some of the sweetest pineapples in the world, so why not share that freshness with everyone, everywhere? What started as a small family stall selling whole pineapples at a weekend market has grown into a full range of pineapple-based food, drinks, and skincare — all rooted in the same orchard where it began.

Every product we make starts with fruit harvested at peak ripeness from trusted local farms. We slice, press, ferment, and bottle in small batches, so what reaches you tastes as close to fresh-cut pineapple as possible — no shortcuts, no artificial flavouring.
