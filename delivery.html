Delivery Info

Everything you need to know about getting your order

Free Delivery ≥ RM 60

Spend RM 60 or more in a single order and delivery is on us, anywhere in Peninsular Malaysia.

1–3 Working Days

Most orders are packed within 24 hours and delivered within 1–3 working days of dispatch.

Chilled Packaging

Fresh and chilled items are packed with ice gel and insulation to keep them cold in transit.

Delivery FAQ

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link by email and SMS. You can also check your order status anytime under My Account → Order History.

Address changes are possible only before your order is dispatched. Contact us as soon as possible at hello@pinenas.com or call +60 12-345 6789.

Our courier partners deliver Monday to Saturday. Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays will be processed on the next working day.

We're sorry if that happens! Please refer to our Returns Policy for how to request a replacement or refund.
